The "One Too Many" rule

I genuinely believe that leftover pizza is a feature, not a bug. If this calculator tells you that you need 5.2 pizzas, order 6. It’s always better to have three cold slices in the fridge the next morning than to have one guest standing by an empty box looking sad. Trust me, I've lived through the 12-party "slice drought," and it’s awkward.

How we count kids

Unless you're hosting a sports team of teenagers, kids usually eat about half as much as adults. Our math assumes kids eat 1.5 to 2 slices on average. If it's a toddler party, you can probably drop the count even further, but why risk it? Everyone loves crust.
