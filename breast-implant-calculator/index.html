Finding Your CCs

I genuinely believe that choosing the right implant size is more about geometry than just "a cup size." Your **Breast Base Width (BBW)** is the most important measurement because the implant must sit comfortably within the boundaries of your natural breast tissue to avoid complications.

Volume vs Projection:

  • Moderate Projection: Provides a wider, flatter look, often appearing very natural.
  • High Projection: Projects further out from the chest wall, giving more upper-pole fullness.
  • Ultra High Projection: Maximum forward projection with a narrower base width.

Disclaimer: This tool provides an estimate based on average industry dimensions. Your plastic surgeon will take detailed 3D measurements and perform physical assessments to determine the absolute best size for your anatomy.
