The power of 10 minutes

I genuinely believe the hardest part of meditation is just showing up. In a world of infinite scrolling, sitting still for 10 minutes feels like an eternity. But that's exactly why it works. This timer is designed to be as non-intrusive as possible. No ads, no popups, just a soft visual pulse to remind you to breathe.

Why use a web timer instead of an app?

Apps are designed to keep you on your phone. A web timer allows you to set it on your laptop or tablet, turn off notifications, and truly disconnect from your mobile device.

How often should I meditate?

Consistency beats intensity. 5 minutes every day is far better than 60 minutes once a week. Start small and let the habit grow naturally.
