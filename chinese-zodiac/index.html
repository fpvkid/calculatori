The 12 Animals of the Moon

I genuinely believe that the Chinese Zodiac offers a fascinating alternative to Western astrology. Legend has it that the Great Jade Emperor invited all the animals of the kingdom to a race. The first twelve to finish—the Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Pig—earned a place in the calendar.
