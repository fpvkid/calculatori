Is Affirm a good deal?

I genuinely believe that Affirm is a double-edged sword. If you get the 0% APR offer, it’s a brilliant way to keep your cash in the bank. But if they offer you 30%, you are essentially taking out a high-interest credit card loan for a consumer purchase. Always check the total interest cost before you commit.

0% APR Offers: These are usually sponsored by the merchant (like Peloton or Apple) to encourage you to buy. They are great as long as you make your payments on time. Affirm doesn't charge "late fees," but missing payments will hurt your credit score and your ability to use the service in the future.

Affirm Tips:

  • Autopay: Turn it on immediately. It’s the only way to ensure you never miss a window.
  • Check the APR: Affirm isn't one rate for everyone. They check your credit "softly" during checkout to determine if you get 10% or 30%.
  • Returns: If you return an item, you still get your principal back, but you might not get back the interest you've already paid.
