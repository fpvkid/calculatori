The "2080" Rule

I genuinely believe that visualizing your income in different formats helps with budgeting. In the professional world, a common shortcut to convert hourly to salary is to multiply your rate by 2000 (roughly 50 weeks) or 2080 (exactly 52 weeks at 40 hours). This gives you a quick baseline for negotiations.

Gross vs Net: Remember that this calculator shows your "Gross" income—this is the total amount before any federal, state, or social security taxes are deducted. Your actual take-home pay will be lower.

Salary Benchmarks:

  • $25/hr: ~$52,000 per year (Median US individual income range).
  • $50/hr: ~$104,000 per year (The "Six Figure" milestone).
  • $75/hr: ~$156,000 per year.
