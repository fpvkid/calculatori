The "Math Anxiety" at the store

I genuinely hate the pressure of trying to figure out if "$50 with 15% off and a $5 coupon" is a better deal than "$40 flat." Stores bank on you being tired and just guessing. This calculator is designed to be mobile-first so you can pull it up while standing in the aisle, type in the number, and know exactly what's coming out of your bank account.

Note on "Stacking": Most stores calculate sales tax *after* the discount is applied. Our math follows this standard retail logic. If you have multiple coupons, apply the biggest percentage first, then subtract any flat-rate dollars at the end.
