The Truth About Acai Bowls

I genuinely believe that Acai bowls are the greatest marketing win of the 21st century. Acai itself is a low-sugar berry packed with antioxidants. But by the time it’s blended with apple juice and topped with honey, granola, and nut butter, a single bowl can easily hit 1,000 calories and 80g of sugar.

How to keep it healthy: Skip the juice in the base and use unsweetened almond milk. Go heavy on the seeds (hemp/chia) for protein and fiber, and keep the granola to a small sprinkle. Most of the calories come from the nut butters and the granola—use them wisely!

Nutrition Milestones:

  • Standard 16oz Bowl: ~400-600 calories.
  • With Nut Butter & Honey: ~700-900 calories.
  • Daily Sugar: A large bowl often exceeds the WHO recommended daily sugar intake in one sitting.
