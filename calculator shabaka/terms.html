What this tool does

RawPawsCalc is a free online calculator that estimates daily raw food portions for dogs using the 80/10/10 ratio (prey model raw). The calculations are based on commonly cited feeding guidelines in the raw feeding community.

Not veterinary advice

This calculator is an educational tool. It does not replace professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Every dog is different. Factors like breed, metabolism, health conditions, and medications can change how much a dog should eat. Before starting a raw diet, especially for puppies or dogs with health issues, consult a veterinarian or qualified canine nutritionist.

Accuracy

We do our best to keep the calculations accurate and consistent with widely accepted raw feeding practices. However, we make no guarantees about the results. The output should be treated as a starting point, not a prescription. You are responsible for monitoring your dog's weight and condition and adjusting amounts accordingly.
