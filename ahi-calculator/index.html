What is the STOP-BANG score?

I genuinely believe that Sleep Apnea is the silent killer of productivity. Most people think they are "just bad sleepers," but if you stop breathing 15 times an hour, your brain is essentially suffocating all night. This STOP-BANG test is the same one used by doctors to decide if a patient needs an expensive sleep study.

Low Risk (0-2): You're likely fine, but check your pillow height. Intermediate Risk (3-4): You should mention this to your doctor. High Risk (5-8): This is a serious signal. Most people in this range have moderate to severe sleep apnea.

Disclaimer: This screening is for educational purposes and is not a medical diagnosis. If you are concerned about your sleep, please consult a healthcare professional.
