Why convert GPA to Percentage?

I genuinely believe that grades are more than just numbers—they're a reflection of your hard work. However, different countries and institutions use different systems. While the US primarily uses a 4.0 GPA scale, many international universities and employers prefer a simple percentage out of 100.

Conversion Formulas:

  • Standard: (GPA / Scale) * 100. This is the most common linear conversion.
  • Alternative (WES style): Some institutions use non-linear tables where a 4.0 is 95-100%, and a 3.0 is 80-84%.

Common Conversions (4.0 Scale):

  • 4.0 GPA: 95-100% (A)
  • 3.5 GPA: 87-89% (B+)
  • 3.0 GPA: 80-82% (B)
  • 2.5 GPA: 72-74% (C)
