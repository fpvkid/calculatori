The Language of Dots and Dashes

I genuinely believe that Morse code is the most resilient form of communication ever invented. It was designed to be sent via light, sound, or electricity, and it remains a backup standard for aviators and the military today. Developed in the 1830s by Samuel Morse, it changed the world by making long-distance communication instant.
