Is it time for new windows?

I genuinely believe that windows are the most underrated part of a home's value. If you can feel a draft or see fog between the glass panes, your windows aren't just ugly—they are actively stealing money from your bank account through your energy bill.

Vinyl vs Wood: Vinyl is the king of value. It doesn't rot, never needs paint, and costs about half of what wood does. Wood is beautiful and lasts longer if maintained, but unless you live in a historic district, vinyl is almost always the smarter financial choice.

Cost Factors:

  • Low-E Coating: Adds about 10-15% to the cost but saves double that in heating/cooling.
  • Second Floor: Labor costs go up if installers need ladders or scaffolding.
  • Disposal: Don't forget to ask if your contractor includes the cost of hauling away your old crusty windows!
