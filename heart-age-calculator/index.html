What does "Heart Age" mean?

I genuinely believe that "heart age" is a much better way to talk about health than "percentage risk." Telling someone they have a 5% risk of a heart attack in 10 years sounds abstract. Telling a 35-year-old they have the heart of a 50-year-old is a wake-up call.

Can you reverse it? Absolutely. Unlike your birthday, your heart age can go backward. Lowering your blood pressure by just 10 points or quitting smoking can shave 5-10 years off your heart's biological age in just a few months.
