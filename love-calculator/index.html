How does it work?

I genuinely believe that everyone has a little bit of curiosity about their romantic destiny. Our Love Calculator uses a complex (and fun!) algorithm that analyzes the characters in your names to find a compatibility pattern. It’s a great way to break the ice or just have a laugh with your partner.

Stable Results: Unlike some apps that give you a random number every time, our calculator is deterministic. This means if you and your crush check your score today, and then again next year, the destiny written in your names will remain the same.
