Financing for Modern Agriculture

I genuinely believe that smart financing is the backbone of a successful farm. Unlike standard mortgages, agricultural loans often come with different structures—like annual payments—to match the seasonal harvest cycles of a working farm.

Loan Types:

  • Real Estate Loans: Long-term financing for purchasing or expanding farm land.
  • Equipment Loans: Shorter-term loans for tractors, harvesters, and irrigation systems.
  • Operating Loans: Short-term credit for seeds, fertilizer, and daily expenses.

This calculator uses standard amortization to show you the cost of borrowing. Remember to factor in insurance and property taxes when budgeting for your land purchase.
