Choosing the Perfect Name

I genuinely believe that a name is the first gift you give your child. It carries history, hope, and personality. Whether you are looking for a family heirloom name or something completely new and avant-garde, the process should be fun and meaningful.

Naming Trends: In recent years, we've seen a massive shift toward "Vintage Revival" names like Eleanor, Theodore, and Arthur. Simultaneously, gender-neutral names like Avery, Rowan, and Charlie continue to rise in popularity for their modern and inclusive feel.

Naming Tips:

  • The Initials Test: Write out the full name and initials to ensure they don't spell anything embarrassing.
  • The Shout Test: Practice shouting the name across the house or a playground to see how it feels.
  • Sibling Harmony: Consider how the new name sounds alongside your existing children's names.
  • Meaning: Research the etymology. A name that means "Strength" or "Light" can provide a beautiful narrative for your child's life.
