The hidden cost of "Syncs"

I find it fascinating (and terrifying) that most companies have strict budgets for software or office snacks, but they let people spend hundreds of dollars in salary costs on a single hour of purposeless chatter. If you have 8 people in a room making $100k each, that hour literally cost the company nearly $500. Was that meeting worth a decent hotel night or a new laptop? Usually, no.

Use this ticker offensively. Put it on the big screen during your next standup. I promise you, people will start getting to the point a lot faster when they see the dollar figure ticking up every second.
