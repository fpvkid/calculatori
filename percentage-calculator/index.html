How do I calculate percent increase manually?

To calculate percentage increase: 1. Subtract the original number from the new number. 2. Divide that result by the original number. 3. Multiply by 100.

What is 20% off $100?

It's $80. You take the original price ($100), multiply it by 0.20 to get the discount ($20), and subtract that from the original ($80).
