Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2025

calcsfor.life ("we", "the site") is a collection of free browser-based calculators and timers. This page explains what data we collect and how it's used.

What we collect

All calculators run entirely in your browser. The values you enter are processed locally and are never sent to our servers. We don't store any personally identifiable information.

Cookies and analytics

We may use basic analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand how many people visit the site and which pages they look at. These tools may place cookies on your browser. You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Third-party advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense or similar advertising networks. These services may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies lets it and its partners serve ads to you based on your browsing patterns. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party links

The site may link to external resources. We're not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
